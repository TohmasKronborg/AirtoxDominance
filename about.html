ABOUT

WHAT IS AIRTOX DOMINANCE FC

ADFC, short for Airtox Dominance Fighting Championship, was established by three-time Olympian and UFC veteran Mark O. Madsen. Airtox Dominance FC’s vision is to establish Europe’s premier fight promotion, hosting multiple Airtox Dominance FC events annually. Airtox Dominance FC is dedicated to advancing the sport of MMA in Europe, creating exceptional experiences for fighters and fans alike.

WHY AIRTOX DOMINANCE FC

MMA, or Mixed Martial Arts, is the world’s fastest-growing sport, and we see enormous potential in the European MMA scene. Airtox Dominance FC aims to create a platform that matches this growth, promotes the sport in Europe, and provides European talents with an international stage to develop their potential and build their brand.

WHAT DO WE AIM TO ACHIEVE

Thrilling MMA Shows

Airtox Dominance FC is committed to delivering adrenaline-pumping MMA events that captivate spectators and sponsors alike, showcasing top European talent in every fight.

Expanding Our Reach

Starting in Denmark, our vision is to grow into an internationally recognized MMA promotion. With a strong social media presence, we provide a platform for fighters to market themselves while engaging fans with compelling fight narratives.

Unforgettable Fight Experiences

We strive to create a high-energy atmosphere where fights go beyond competition—they become unforgettable moments, building stories that elevate the MMA experience.
